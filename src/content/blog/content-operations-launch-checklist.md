## Checks to finish before launching Content Operations

To minimize rework and maximize efficiency, follow this launch sequence for content operations in Lisbon.

1. **Pilot Phase**: Launch content operations with a small, representative sample of content. This allows for testing, iteration, and gathering feedback before full-scale launch.

2. **Iterate and Improve**: Based on feedback and performance data from the pilot phase, make necessary adjustments to content operations.

3. **Full-Scale Launch**: Once content operations are optimized, launch fully in Lisbon. This ensures a smooth rollout and minimizes the risk of major issues.

## Metrics to watch after launch

After launching content operations in Lisbon, monitor the following metrics to ensure success and identify areas for improvement.

1. **Content Creation Time**: Track how long it takes to create and publish content. This helps identify bottlenecks and optimize workflows.

2. **Content Approval Time**: Monitor the time taken for content approval. This helps ensure a smooth content approval process and identifies any delays.

3. **Content Performance**: Measure content performance using metrics like engagement, shares, and conversions. This helps identify what content resonates with the local audience.

4. **Localization Accuracy**: Ensure content is accurately localized. This includes language accuracy, cultural relevance, and adherence to local regulations.

5. **Team Feedback**: Regularly collect feedback from the content operations team. This helps identify challenges, gather ideas for improvement, and maintain team satisfaction.
